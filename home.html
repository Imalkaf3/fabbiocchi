Exploring Cultures Through Art

Dr. Renée Fabbi.Occhi is an internationally recognized artist whose work bridges continents and cultures. With roots in Paris, Rome, and Tokyo, her paintings explore the intersection of Eastern and Western artistic traditions.

Her unique perspective comes from decades of study and practice across Europe and Asia, resulting in a body of work that is both deeply personal and universally resonant.

"Life and Art Are One!" is not just her motto, but the guiding principle behind every brushstroke.
